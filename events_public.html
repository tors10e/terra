Oct 29th - 31st, 2021 - Food by Eric Pitts

After a long wait, Terra now has a fully permitted kitchen! Thanks to the help of Eric, we are now serving food which is a big step forward for us. Friday and Saturday, because of weddings at the pavilion, we will be hosting tastings and food on the western hillside which requires us to fall back on our limited menu. We will be back at the pavilion on Sunday and will add soup and flat bread pizzas to the menu! Don't forget fall weather is here so bring a jacket!

Nov. 19th, Music by Ryan Little Eagle

This Saturday we have a new artist, Ryan Little Eagle, a Native American singer-songwriter. He plays a "NDN Country" (say it out loud to understand that it's not an acronym) which is a mix of country and First Nations music. Check out his bio at his website.

Nov 22nd, 2021 - Music by Jake Hicks

Jake Hicks will be playing for us on the 22nd of Nov. Come join us for some great music!
