Aug 14, 2021 - Lobster Dog Food Truck and Smoke on the Mountain LRA event

This weekend we will be hosting the Smoke on the Mountain event for the Lake Rabun Association at the pavilion. This means that wine tastings will be served outside on the western meadow. The Lobster Dog Food truck will be there with great food like lobster rolls and stuffed avocados, you can see the whole menu here (Lobster Dog Menu).

For those of you from the Lake Rabun Association that will be attending the Smoke on the Mountain event, welcome! The food sounds amazing and will be held in the pavilion situated on the eastern hillside. Have fun and congratulation on the money you've raised to help out our county!
