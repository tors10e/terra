Events

We provide a 2,700 sq. ft. pavilion with an attached catering kitchen, fireplace, bar, and upstairs suite situated on our eastern hillside. Sunsets fall in the notch between the eastern and western hillsides, providing an excellent evening view from the pavilion lawn and upper vineyard. Views to the north include Rabun Bald (4,696 ft) and Standing Indian (5,499 ft) mountains.

We provide tables and chairs for our guests and can accommodate any reasonable requests.
