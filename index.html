Welcome to Terra Incognita Vineyard

Events

Terra provides a 2,700 sq. ft. pavilion with an attached catering kitchen, bar, and upstairs suite complete with a deck and pull out bed would guests decide to spend the night. Our event lawn has view to the northeast of Rabun Bald and the sun sets in a notch to the illuminating Tray Mountain and surrounding peaks. We provide tables and chairs to our guests and provide can accomodate any reasonable requests our guests may have.
